You’re a multitasking master, so your bag should work just as hard. Lindquist’s advice for you is to “keep the essentials (phone, card holder, lipstick) in a small clutch inside your larger work bag to keep those items organized during the day. Then leave the bulky bag at the office and carry that convenient little clutch to happy hour.” Because we need that glass of wine after work--for our health.
