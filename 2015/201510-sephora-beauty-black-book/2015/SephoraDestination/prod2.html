Contour Like a Pro

GET THE LOOK

From the red carpet to the runway, everyone seems to be on the contouring bandwagon these days. And now the great mysteries of this technique: revealed! First, invest in a contouring palette with a range of natural shades that create depth and structure. Then follow these steps:

PRO Contour Brush #79

 Buy Now
  1. Using the contouring brush, press a bit of your palette’s bronzy powder​(a shade darker than your natural skin tone) onto the sides of your face, right underneath your cheekbones.
  2. Sweep the darker shade forward and up ​toward the center of your face. The brush’s tapered bristles and refined tip offer ideal control for the smaller areas of your features.
  3. With the same brush (it really does it all, doesn’t it?), use long strokes to sweep the same shade along your jawline and then gently blend. ​This will give you that coveted sculpted definition.

Create a Natural Glow

GET THE LOOK

The key to staying young (or at least looking the part)? A radiant, natural glow that brings to mind childhood days spent running around on the playground. Enter highlighting, contouring’s “good” twin and partner in crime. Swirl this refined brush in highlighting powder,​ tap off any excess and get your glow on.

Contour Highlight Brush #80

 Buy Now
WHERE TO APPLY HIGHLIGHTER
  1. CHEEKBONES​ First, smile into a mirror to find the most prominent part of your cheeks (the “apples”). Next, sweep the tapered bristles from the bridge of your nose right up to your hairline, directly above the apples and under your eyes
  2. CUPID’S BOW & CHIN Lightly tap the highlighter above your upper and lower lips.
  3. INNER EYES For extra radiance, carefully dab highlighter at the inner corners of your eyes.

Ace the Smoky Eye

In the Know

News flash: The smoky eye can be conquered. And this season’s fresh take--which includes pretty taupe, bronze and rose-gold shades--is way too pretty to pass up. Make sure you use the fluffy eye-shadow brush for flawless application: It holds on to color so you can press pigment into your creases, and the silky bristles allow you to smudge and blend with total ease.​

PRO Blending Brush #27

 Buy Now
  1. PRESS. Use your brush to press a bronzy shade directly onto your lids, right up to the creases.
  2. HIGHLIGHT. Blend a lighter pearlescent shade from your creases all the way to your brow bones.
  3. SWIRL. Dip the brush into your smokiest shade and focus the pigment on the outer edges of your lids. Instant depth and guaranteed drama.

Create a Subtle Eye

GET THE LOOK

Want to get that gorgeous mug of yours ready for a night out? It really couldn?t be simpler. All it takes is a darker shadow and the right tool. Here’s what to do:

PRO Winged Liner Brush #37

 Buy Now
  1. With your blending brush, swirl a smoky shadow​ onto the outer lids and creases to create depth (and an air of evening mystery).
  2. Grab your winged liner brush ​and gently dip it into the same pigment. (To really amp up your look, use a cream or gel pot liner.)
  3. Line just the top lashes, working from the inner corner to the outer edge (and then out to in). The angled shape and slanted bristles will give you a super­-fine, super-­precise line.
